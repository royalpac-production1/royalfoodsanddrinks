    • Our History

    Royalpac was founded in 2004 by a visionary entrepreneur named Alhaji Adeboye Abdul-Fatai Abimbola. Inspired by his love for international travel and diverse beverrages, lollipops and candies from around the world, he set out to establish a company that would bridge the gap between global markets and bring amzing products to consumers everywhere.
    Initially, Royalpac started as a small trading company, focusing on importing and distributing products like candies, lollipops, cookies, bottle water and much more from various countries Over the years, the company steadily expanded its product range, venturing into pasta production
    By the early 20s, Royalpac had built a strong network of partner companies across the globe, ensuring a steady supply of high-quality products. Their commitment to reliability and excellent customer service earned them a reputation for being a trustworthy partner in the industry.
    In the late 20s, Royalpac made significant advancements in its logistics and distribution capabilities, allowing them to efficiently handle large-scale exports to international markets. The company began collaborating with other beverrages distributors and retailers, establishing long-term partnership that contributed to their growth.
