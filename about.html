    • Our History

    In 2012, a visionary entrepreneur named Adeboye Abimbola Fatai founded “Royal Foods and Drinks ltd.” royal foods and drinks is a manufacturing and an importation company. it involves the importation and manufacturing of edible commodities such as lollipop, bottled water, pure water, sweets, biscuits, cheese balls, chocolate and mayonnaise. Royal foods and drinks started officially in the year 2012 in the territorial zone of benin republic, by then it dealt with only importations of products from a partnered company in togo,lomè called unifood. Tomy, which is the name given to “unifood” products comprised of cooking spices, mayonnaise, and confectioneries . these tomy products were imported and marketed in benin republic by Royal Foods and Drinks, which finally ended up dominating the financial market of the country . After years of continuous financing activities, the founder decided to expand , which led to the construction of factories and other branches of the company in two other different countries, togo and nigeria. the start was difficult but with the grace of god almighty, the company succeeded in dominating the financial market of these various countries till date. Royal Foods and Drinks in the years of growth took a diversion into the manufacturing world. it started with the manufacturing of confectioneries to the manufacturing of bottled water and pure water, now currently on the manufacturing of cheese balls . “royal pac” is the name given to Royal Foods and Drinks self-manufactured products ,and it was officially named by the CEO. Royal Foods and Drinks has only one motive, changing people’s lives” . the company has a continous charity program which has helped a lot of people regain their stand and living a better life , and they are still aiming high to helping more people all over the world. the vision of this great company is broad ,and with consistency and constant growth ,all goals will be achieved.
